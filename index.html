Study System

How Should Students Study? 📚

Foundation System

20 minutes grammar + 20 minutes vocabulary + 20 minutes practice questions.

مناسب للطلاب الضعاف أو البداية.

Exam Preparation System

Practice under time, correct mistakes, then repeat weak topics.

مناسب قبل الامتحان.

Active Recall

Close the book and try to remember. Then check and correct.

لا تقرأ فقط؛ اختبر نفسك.

Mistakes Notebook

Write every repeated mistake and review it before exams.

دفتر الأخطاء سر التحسن.

Weekly Routine

Day 1 grammar, Day 2 vocabulary, Day 3 reading, Day 4 writing, Day 5 exam, Day 6 correction, Day 7 light review.

Smart Advice by Score

Below 50%: review basics. 50–79%: practice more. 80%+: move to advanced questions.
